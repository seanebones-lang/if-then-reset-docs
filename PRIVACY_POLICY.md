## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

If/Then/Reset is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data or GPS coordinates
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Contact lists or photos
- Health data from HealthKit
- Any data that leaves your device

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS secure storage (UserDefaults and local file system):

1. **Mood Entries**
   - Feelings logged (emotion type, intensity)
   - Reset techniques used
   - Timestamps of sessions
   - Optional notes you add
   - **Storage**: Encrypted iOS UserDefaults
   - **Access**: Only you, only on your device

2. **User Preferences**
   - Notification settings (frequency, times)
   - Sound preferences (selected sound, volume)
   - Accessibility settings (reduce motion)
   - **Storage**: iOS UserDefaults
   - **Access**: Only you, only on your device

3. **Subscription Status**
   - Active subscription tier (if any)
   - Trial status
   - **Storage**: iOS UserDefaults (cached from Apple)
   - **Source**: Apple StoreKit (managed by Apple)

4. **App Settings**
   - Onboarding completion status
   - Theme preferences
   - **Storage**: iOS UserDefaults
   - **Access**: Only you, only on your device

### Data Managed by Apple

The following data is managed exclusively by Apple through their systems:

1. **Subscription Purchases**
   - Purchase history
   - Payment information
   - Subscription status
   - **Managed by**: Apple Inc. via StoreKit
   - **Privacy Policy**: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

2. **App Store Account**
   - Apple ID
   - Family Sharing settings
   - **Managed by**: Apple Inc.
   - **Privacy Policy**: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All personal data is stored on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms:

- **Encryption**: iOS automatically encrypts data when device is locked
- **Backup**: Data included in iCloud/iTunes backups (encrypted)
- **Access Control**: Protected by iOS security and your device passcode
- **Deletion**: All data deleted when you delete the app

### No Cloud Storage

We do not use any cloud storage services. Your data never leaves your device except:
- When you manually export data (your choice)
- When included in iOS device backups (managed by Apple)

### Data Retention

- **Mood Entries**: Stored indefinitely on your device until you delete them
- **Preferences**: Stored until you delete the app or reset settings
- **Subscription Status**: Cached locally, source of truth is Apple's servers

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data

All your data is stored on your device. You can:
- View mood entries in the History tab
- Export data as JSON from Settings
- Review preferences in Settings

### Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:
- **Individual Entries**: Swipe to delete in History view
- **All Data**: Use "Delete All Data" in Settings → Privacy
- **Complete Removal**: Delete the app from your device

### Control Notifications

You can control notifications:
- Enable/disable in Settings → Notifications
- Change frequency (none, once, twice, three times daily)
- Customize notification times
- Revoke notification permission in iOS Settings

### Export Your Data

You can export your mood entries:
- Settings → Privacy → Export Data
- Exports as JSON file
- Share via iOS share sheet
- No data sent to our servers
