## Getting Started

### First-Time Setup

1. **Download the App**
   - Open App Store on your iPhone or iPad
   - Search for "If/Then/Reset"
   - Tap "Get" to download and install

2. **Complete Onboarding**
   - Open the app for the first time
   - Read through the welcome screens
   - Review the medical disclaimer
   - Learn about the IF/THEN/RESET process

3. **Optional: Start Free Trial**
   - Tap "Start Free Trial" to access premium features
   - 7-day trial, no charges during trial period
   - Cancel anytime before trial ends to avoid charges

### How to Use the App

**Basic Flow**:
1. **IF**: Tap "Start Reset" → Select how you're feeling → Rate intensity (1-10)
2. **THEN**: Follow breathing exercise → Optional: Play ambient sound
3. **RESET**: Choose a physical reset technique → Follow instructions
4. **Complete**: Add optional notes → View completion affirmation

**View History**:
- Tap "History" tab to see past reset sessions
- Swipe left on any entry to delete
- View statistics and insights

**Customize Settings**:
- Tap "Settings" tab
- Configure notifications, sounds, and preferences
- Manage subscription and privacy options

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open or Crashes

**Solution 1: Force Quit and Restart**
1. Swipe up from bottom of screen (or double-click Home button)
2. Find If/Then/Reset
3. Swipe up to close
4. Reopen the app

**Solution 2: Restart Your Device**
1. Hold Power button until "slide to power off" appears
2. Slide to turn off
3. Wait 30 seconds
4. Turn device back on

**Solution 3: Update the App**
1. Open App Store
2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
3. Scroll to If/Then/Reset
4. Tap "Update" if available

**Solution 4: Reinstall the App**
1. Press and hold app icon
2. Tap "Remove App" → "Delete App"
3. Reinstall from App Store
4. Note: This will delete your local data

### Sounds Won't Play

**Check Volume**:
- Ensure device is not on silent mode (check switch on side)
- Increase volume using volume buttons
- Check volume in app settings

**Check Permissions**:
- Settings → If/Then/Reset → ensure permissions are enabled

**Try Different Sound**:
- Some sounds may not work if files are corrupted
- Try a different ambient sound
- If none work, reinstall the app

**Background Audio Issues**:
- Ensure Low Power Mode is off (Settings → Battery)
- Close other audio apps
- Restart the app

### Notifications Not Working

**Check Authorization**:
1. iOS Settings → Notifications → If/Then/Reset
2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is ON
3. Enable Banners, Sounds, and Badges

**Check App Settings**:
1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Notifications
3. Ensure frequency is not set to "None"
4. Verify notification times are set

**Check Do Not Disturb**:
- Swipe down from top right (Control Center)
- Ensure Focus mode is not blocking notifications

**Reschedule Notifications**:
1. Settings → Notifications → Toggle frequency off then on
2. This will reschedule all notifications

### Subscription Issues

See [Subscription Help](#subscription-help) section below.

### Data Not Saving

**Check Storage**:
- Settings → General → iPhone Storage
- Ensure you have available storage space
- If low, delete unused apps or media

**Force Save**:
- Complete a reset session fully
- Wait for completion screen
- Don't force quit immediately after saving

**Export Data as Backup**:
- Settings → Privacy → Export Data
- Save JSON file to Files app or email yourself

### App Running Slowly

**Close Background Apps**:
- Swipe up from bottom to see all open apps
- Close unnecessary apps

**Clear Cache**:
- Delete and reinstall the app (note: deletes local data)
- Export data first if you want to keep it

**Check iOS Version**:
- Settings → General → Software Update
- Update to latest iOS version
- App requires iOS 17.0 or later

## Subscription Help

### How to Subscribe

1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Tap any premium feature (sounds, insights, etc.)
3. Tap "Start Free Trial" or "Subscribe"
4. Choose Monthly ($3.99/month) or Yearly ($39.99/year)
5. Confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or password
6. 7-day free trial begins (new subscribers only)

### How to Cancel Subscription

**Method 1: iOS Settings**
1. Open iOS Settings
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "If/Then/Reset"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
6. Confirm cancellation

**Method 2: In-App**
1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Subscription
3. Tap "Manage Subscription"
4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Important Notes**:
- Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
- You keep access until period ends
- No refunds for unused time
- Can resubscribe anytime

### How to Restore Purchases

If you subscribed on another device or reinstalled the app:

1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Subscription
3. Tap "Restore Purchases"
4. Wait for verification (may take 10-30 seconds)
5. Premium features will unlock if subscription is active

**Troubleshooting Restore**:
- Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase
- Check that subscription is still active (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions)
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support if issue persists

### Free Trial Questions

**Q: How long is the free trial?**  
A: 7 days from the moment you subscribe.

**Q: Will I be charged during the trial?**  
A: No. Charges begin only after the trial ends.

**Q: How do I cancel before being charged?**  
A: Cancel anytime during the 7-day trial period using the steps above.

**Q: Can I get another free trial?**  
A: No. Free trials are available once per Apple ID per subscription group.

**Q: What happens when the trial ends?**  
A: Subscription automatically converts to paid subscription at regular price.

### Refund Requests

All refunds are handled by Apple:

1. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find If/Then/Reset subscription charge
4. Tap "Report a Problem"
5. Select reason and submit request

Or contact Apple Support:
- https://support.apple.com/billing
- Refunds are at Apple's discretion
- We cannot process refunds directly

### Family Sharing

Subscriptions support Apple Family Sharing:

**To Share Your Subscription**:
1. Ensure Family Sharing is set up (Settings → Apple ID → Family Sharing)
2. Your subscription automatically shares with family members
3. Up to 5 family members can access premium features

**To Use Shared Subscription**:
1. Be part of a Family Sharing group
2. Open If/Then/Reset
3. Premium features automatically unlock
4. No separate subscription needed

**Note**: Only the primary subscriber is charged.

## Technical Issues

### Compatibility

**Supported Devices**:
- iPhone (iOS 17.0 or later)
- iPad (iPadOS 17.0 or later)

**Not Supported**:
- Apple Watch (no companion app yet)
- Mac (no macOS version)
- Android devices

**Check Your iOS Version**:
- Settings → General → About → Software Version
- Update if below iOS 17.0

### Performance Issues

**App Freezes or Lags**:
1. Force quit and reopen
2. Restart device
3. Update to latest app version
4. Ensure iOS is up to date
5. Free up device storage space

**Battery Drain**:
- Background audio uses battery
- Stop sounds when not in use
- Reduce notification frequency
- Enable Low Power Mode if needed

**Storage Space**:
- App size: ~50 MB
- Sound files: ~8 MB
- User data: Minimal (< 1 MB typically)

### Sync Issues

**Note**: The app does NOT sync between devices.
- All data is stored locally on each device
- Subscriptions sync via Apple (automatic)
- Mood entries do NOT sync between devices

**To Transfer Data**:
1. Export data from old device (Settings → Privacy → Export)
2. Transfer JSON file to new device
3. Import on new device (Settings → Privacy → Import)

## Privacy & Data

### What Data is Collected?

**Short Answer**: None. All data stays on your device.

**Long Answer**:
- No personal information collected
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No data transmitted to our servers
- Mood entries stored locally only
- Subscription status managed by Apple

See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for complete details.

### How to Export Your Data

1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Privacy → Export Data
3. Tap "Export as JSON"
4. Choose where to save (Files, Email, etc.)
5. Data exports as JSON file

**What's Included**:
- All mood entries
- Timestamps and feelings
- Reset techniques used
- Optional notes

**What's NOT Included**:
- Subscription status (managed by Apple)
- App settings (device-specific)

### How to Delete Your Data

**Delete Individual Entries**:
1. History tab
2. Swipe left on entry
3. Tap "Delete"

**Delete All Data**:
1. Settings → Privacy
2. Tap "Delete All Data"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Cannot be undone

**Complete Removal**:
- Delete the app from your device
- All local data is permanently deleted
- Subscription continues until cancelled

### Data Security

**How We Protect Your Data**:
- All data stored locally on your device
- iOS encrypts data when device is locked
- Protected by your device passcode/biometrics
- No cloud storage or transmission

**Your Responsibility**:
- Use a strong device passcode
- Enable Face ID or Touch ID
- Don't share your device with others
- Keep iOS updated for security patches

## Emergency Resources

### CRITICAL: If You Are in Crisis

**If/Then/Reset is NOT a crisis service or emergency tool.**

If you are experiencing a mental health emergency or having thoughts of self-harm:

### United States

**Emergency Services**: 911  
**Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**: 988 (call or text)  
**Crisis Text Line**: Text "HELLO" to 741741  
**SAMHSA National Helpline**: 1-800-662-4357 (24/7, free, confidential)

### International Resources

**International Association for Suicide Prevention**:  
https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/

**Befrienders Worldwide**:  
https://www.befrienders.org/

### Additional Mental Health Resources

**National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)**:  
1-800-950-6264  
https://www.nami.org/

**Anxiety and Depression Association of America (ADAA)**:  
https://adaa.org/

**Mental Health America**:  
https://www.mhanational.org/

**SAMHSA Treatment Locator**:  
https://findtreatment.samhsa.gov/

### When to Seek Professional Help

Consult a mental health professional if you experience:
- Persistent feelings of sadness, anxiety, or hopelessness
- Difficulty functioning in daily life
- Changes in sleep, appetite, or energy
- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- Substance abuse issues
- Trauma or PTSD symptoms
- Relationship or family problems

**Remember**: If/Then/Reset is a self-help tool, not a replacement for professional care.
