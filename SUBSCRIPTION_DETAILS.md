## Free Trial

### Eligibility

- Available to new subscribers only
- One trial per Apple ID per subscription group
- Must not have previously subscribed to If/Then/Reset premium

### How It Works

1. **Day 0**: Subscribe and trial begins immediately
2. **Days 1-7**: Full access to all premium features, no charges
3. **Day 7**: Trial ends, subscription converts to paid automatically
4. **Day 8+**: Regular billing begins

### Important Notes

- **No Charges During Trial**: You will not be charged during the 7-day trial period
- **Cancel Anytime**: Cancel during trial to avoid charges
- **Automatic Conversion**: If not cancelled, trial converts to paid subscription
- **No Partial Refunds**: If you cancel after trial ends, no refund for unused time

### How to Cancel During Trial

**iOS Settings Method**:
1. Open Settings app
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "If/Then/Reset"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
6. Confirm cancellation

**In-App Method**:
1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Subscription
3. Tap "Manage Subscription"
4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Result**: You keep access until trial ends, then subscription does not renew.

- Existing subscriptions honored until renewal

## Managing Your Subscription

### How to Subscribe

**In-App**:
1. Tap any premium feature (sounds, insights, etc.)
2. Tap "Start Free Trial" or "Subscribe"
3. Choose Monthly or Yearly
4. Confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or password
5. Trial begins immediately (if eligible)

**App Store**:
1. Open App Store
2. Search "If/Then/Reset"
3. Scroll to "In-App Purchases"
4. Select subscription plan
5. Complete purchase

### How to Cancel

**iOS Settings** (Recommended):
1. Open Settings app
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select "If/Then/Reset"
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
6. Confirm cancellation

**In-App**:
1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Subscription
3. Tap "Manage Subscription"
4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Important**:
- Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
- You retain access until period ends
- No refunds for unused time
- Can resubscribe anytime

### How to Change Plans

**Switch Between Monthly and Yearly**:
1. iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
2. Select "If/Then/Reset"
3. Choose different plan
4. Confirm change

**Notes**:
- Change takes effect at next renewal
- No partial refunds when switching
- Can upgrade or downgrade anytime

### How to Restore Purchases

If you subscribed on another device or reinstalled the app:

1. Open If/Then/Reset
2. Settings → Subscription
3. Tap "Restore Purchases"
4. Wait for verification (10-30 seconds)
5. Premium features unlock automatically

**Troubleshooting**:
- Ensure same Apple ID used to purchase
- Check subscription is active (Settings → Subscriptions)
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support if issue persists

## Family Sharing

### How It Works

Share your subscription with up to 5 family members:
- **Primary Subscriber**: Pays for subscription
- **Family Members**: Get free access to premium features
- **Privacy**: Each person's data stays private on their device
- **Management**: Controlled through Apple Family Sharing

### Setting Up Family Sharing

**Prerequisites**:
1. Set up Apple Family Sharing (Settings → [Your Name] → Family Sharing)
2. Add family members to your family group
3. Subscribe to If/Then/Reset premium

**Automatic Sharing**:
- Subscription automatically shares with family members
- No additional setup required in If/Then/Reset
- Family members download app and sign in with their Apple ID

### Using Shared Subscription

**For Family Members**:
1. Download If/Then/Reset from App Store
2. Open the app
3. Premium features automatically unlock
4. No separate subscription needed

**Notes**:
- All family members must be in same Family Sharing group
- Only primary subscriber can cancel subscription
- If primary cancels, all family members lose access

### Refund Policy

Refunds are handled exclusively by Apple:
- We cannot process refunds directly
- All refund requests must go through Apple
- Refunds are at Apple's sole discretion
- Apple's refund policy applies

### How to Request Refund

**Method 1: Report a Problem**
1. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find If/Then/Reset subscription charge
4. Tap "Report a Problem"
5. Select reason (e.g., "I didn't mean to purchase this")
6. Submit request

**Method 2: Apple Support**
1. Visit https://support.apple.com/billing
2. Contact Apple Support
3. Explain your situation
4. Apple will review your request

**Processing Time**:
- Refunds typically processed within 48 hours
- Refund appears on original payment method
- May take 3-5 business days to reflect in account

### Refund Eligibility

Apple may grant refunds for:
- Accidental purchases
- Technical issues preventing use
- Billing errors
- Other valid reasons at Apple's discretion

Apple typically does NOT refund:
- Change of mind after trial period
- Unused time on active subscription
- Failure to cancel before renewal
